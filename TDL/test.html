What we offer..

Join The Engaged Research Lab

Engaged Research Lab targets three areas - industry partnership, high-impact research, and student engagement.

For Industry Partners

Engaged Research Lab provides organisations opportunities to identify and recruit highly qualified students with strong business competencies.

For Students

Students gain intership opportunities, access to profesisonal networks, and opportunity to work on research projects with local and globaal industry, government, and community partners.

Research Partnership

The Lab offers many topical areas of research for Doctoral (PhD) and Masters studets.
