Hi there, I am Manoj!

I am an Associate Professor in the Discipline of Business Information Systems at The University of Sydney Business School. My research interests focus primarily in areas of Data Science and emerging technologies. I investigate applications of knowledge and data management, semantic technologies and machine learning in across diverse research settings such as public health, education, and ecommerce. I am also actively involved in ICT for Developing Countries (ICT4D) where I explore unconventional applications of technological solutions to address societal challenges.
