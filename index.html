Hi there, I am Manoj!

I am an Associate Professor in the Discipline of Business Information Systems at The University of Sydney Business School. My research interests focus primarily in areas of Data Science and emerging technologies. I investigate applications of knowledge and data management, semantic technologies and machine learning in across diverse research settings such as public health, education, and ecommerce.

I am also actively involved in ICT for Developing Countries (ICT4D) where I explore unconventional applications of technological solutions to address societal challenges. You can find more about some of the work here: Techies Without Borders.

Research

Data Science

I am particularly interested in investigating the integration of machine learning algorithms in resource-constrained devices and sensors. In the past, I has investigated analytical maturity of businesses to support knowledge discovery using data analytics, solutions for data mining model management for self-service knowledge discovery, and the use of ontologies for knowledge modeling in domains such as e-government and e-commerce.
